argument-hint: "--author \"Poet Name\" [theme] [--count N] [--period \"time/setting\"] [--style \"early/late/specific phase\"] [--characters \"char descriptions\"]"

Generate a poetry collection from: $ARGUMENTS

1. **Calibrate for verse** — line architecture, stanza rhythm, diction, sound (rhyme, meter, alliteration), imagery, compression, opening/closing patterns, typography, relationship to form. Test a stanza (3–5 lines); recalibrate until recognizable.
2. **Plan** — poem list with title/subject/form/role in arc, emotional trajectory, formal variety, 3–5 recurring image motifs. If `--characters` provided, weave through as speakers or presences.
3. **Write** — parallel agents. The line is the unit. Sound matters. Every word earns its place. Match where this poet turns.
4. **Output** — save as `[title-kebab-case].md`. Line breaks preserved, stanzas separated by blank lines, poems by `---`. Reproduce the poet's typography. Header/disclaimer as in tale.
