Crawl a blog or writings page and weave articles into thematic threads: $ARGUMENTS
1. Discovers all articles from the index page
2. Groups articles into 3-7 thematic threads based on recurring ideas
3. Fetches full articles and extracts key passages per thread
4. Orders excerpts by conceptual progression, not chronology
5. Writes margin annotations emphasizing cross-article and cross-thread connections
6. Generates a markdown file organized by threads with excerpts and annotations
